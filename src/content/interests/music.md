The only instrument I can really play is the trumpet, which was my band instrument. It has been
a few years since I opened the case, now worried there could be scary life forms in there.

I've dabbled with piano, drums, guitar, and bass, but currently own none of those.

Mostly I listen to music, but have a particular interest in knowing the stories behind bands and
artists, as much as the music itself.

My favourite artists include [Sparks](https://allsparks.com) and [The Tragically Hip](http://thehip.com).
